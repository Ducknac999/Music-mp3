📥 How to save your MP3s

Your MP3 files will download one by one to your phone's Downloads folder. Each file has the cover art and artist name embedded.

To move them to a Music MP3 folder:
Open your file manager → Downloads → move the MP3 files to a folder named "Music MP3".
